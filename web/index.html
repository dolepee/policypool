Pools that can say no.

PolicyPool lets each v4 pool publish the swap-size and daily-volume covenant its liquidity will accept. The covenant is enforced inside beforeSwap, before the pool consumes the trade.

Hook mechanism

A covenant check in the swap path.

PolicyPool keeps the submission narrow: one Hook, one callback, two pool-level limits. No oracle, no offchain matcher, no governance layer. 

if amountIn > maxSwapAmount:
  revert PolicyBlocked("MAX_SWAP_EXCEEDED")

if spentToday + amountIn > dailyCap:
  revert PolicyBlocked("DAILY_CAP_EXCEEDED")

spentToday += amountIn
emit SwapAccepted(poolId, trader, amountIn)
